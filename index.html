Danielle's Top 10 TV Shows
This is a static demo project I created based on my favourite games. Check out Media Masterlist to make a list of your own.
Title Score Best Character Genre Review
Derry Girls
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Orla Period Teen Sitcom Despite taking place in Northern Ireland in the 1990s, this is a fun and surprisingly relatable watch for the modern day Newfoundlander.
Dead to Me
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Judy Black Comedy This dark comedic drama explores themes of grief, forgiveness, and redemption, offering lots of laughs and tears along the way.
The Good Place
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Michael Fantasy Comedy What does it mean to be good? What do we owe each other? The Good Place answers these questions with a dash of philosophy and fun.
The Office (US)
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Creed Mockumentary Sitcom The Office is a comedy classic complete with an amazing roster of hilarious actors and writers with her companion Puppycat.
Bee and Puppycat
⭐⭐⭐⭐
Puppycat Surreal Cartoon This cute yet surreal animated series stars a magical girl named Bee who goes on whimsical adventures.
Gravity Falls
⭐⭐⭐⭐
Grunkle Stan Mystery Comedy Gravity Falls is a wholesome mystery series with a supernatural twist. Its clever writing can be enjoyed by all ages.
Son of a Critch
⭐⭐⭐⭐
The Grandpa Sitcom I enjoyed this semi-autobiographical series a lot more than I thought I would. I'd argue it's the best show that takes place in Newfoundland.
Misfits
⭐⭐⭐⭐
Nathan British Supernatural Comedy Drama An absurd outlandish adventure featuring memorable characters, amazing music, and some odd writing choices.
Monk
⭐⭐⭐
Sharona Comedic Procedural Mystery Drama Monk is predictable and repetitive yet surprisingly charming. The characters grow on you far more than you'd expect.
Miraculous Ladybug
⭐⭐⭐
Kagami Superhero Cartoon Despite its objectively mediocre writing, Miraculous Ladybug always manages to put me in a good mood. Watching it is my favourite way to practice my French.
