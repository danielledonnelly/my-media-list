Danielle's Top 10 Favourite Games
This is a static demo project I created based on some of my favourite games. Check out the Media Masterlist site to make a list of your own.
Title Score Platform Genre Status Review
Dicey Dungeons
Nintendo Switch Indie Deckbuilder Completed This charming deckbuilder is a satisfying game that never fails to make me smile.
Genshin Impact
PS5 Gacha RPG Currently playing I've spent over 2000 hours on this game. It's part of my daily routine to play it each morning while I'm stationary cycling.
Nintendogs
Nintendo Simulation Currently re-playing This is a childhood favourite of mine that I like to re-visit every now and then.
Butterfly Soup
PC Visual Novel Currently playing I highly recommend checking out this heart-warming love story that's free to download on Itch.io.
Stardew Valley
PC Farming Sim Currently re-playing This is an amazing and expansive indie simulation game that was created entirely by one person.
Life is Strange
PS4 Narrative Adventure Completed I learned a lot of things about myself when I played this game for the first time as a teenager. It's a solid game with some interesting themes.
Atelier Sophie: Alchemist of the Mysterious Book
PS4 JRPG Completed This cutesy JRPG lets you play as an alchemist that runs a magic shop. It's a lot of fun getting to make potions, charms, and other magical items.
Life is Strange: True Colors
PS5 Narrative Adventure Completed This stellar spin-off game focuses on a young woman with supernatural powers investigating a small-town mystery.
Saints Row: the Third
PS4 Action Adventure Completed The Saints Row series always feels like an over the top parody of GTA; it's extremely goofy and fun!
MySims Kingdom
Wii Simulation Completed MySims Kingdom was one of the first games I got really invested in as a child. I love the cutesy art style and character customization.
